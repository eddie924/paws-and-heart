About Paws & Hearts

We are a community-driven rescue dedicated to helping pets find caring homes. Every animal in our care receives love, training, and support until they are adopted.

Our Mission

To make pet adoption simple, affordable, and joyful. We connect families with wonderful companions and help pets thrive in their new homes.

Who We Are

Our team is made up of volunteers, adoption coordinators, and animal lovers who care deeply about every pet in our shelter.
